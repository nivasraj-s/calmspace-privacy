Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how CalmSpace (the "App") collects, uses, and shares information. Developer: Nivasraj. Contact: s.nivasraj.s@gmail.com.

1. Summary

Short version: CalmSpace does not collect personal information from users. The app displays ads and uses notifications. Some third-party services (Google/Firebase) may automatically collect limited device identifiers and crash/usage analytics as described below.

2. Information the app DOES NOT collect

  • No user account information (no login, no email sign-in).
  • No contact lists, no location tracking, no photos, and no personal messages.
  • No payment information is collected by the app (if payments are used, they are handled by Play Store or other providers).

3. Third-party services & automatic data collected

Although the app itself does not collect personal data, the following third-party SDKs used by the app may automatically collect limited device data:

  • Google AdMob — used to display ads. Ad SDKs may collect device identifiers and other advertising-related information to serve ads and measure performance. For details and opt-out options, see Google Ads Settings: adssettings.google.com.
  • Firebase (Realtime Database) — used only to perform occasional reads (for example: remote app version checks). These reads use short-lived requests and do not open persistent realtime listeners. Firebase may collect crash reports and analytics if you enable those SDKs; these are handled by Firebase and Google according to their policies.

Note: SDK-collected data is collected by the SDK providers and is not considered developer-collected personal data for the purposes of the Play Console Data Safety form.

4. How we use information

  • To display targeted or non-targeted ads via AdMob.
  • To check the current app version from the database for update prompts (this uses a single short read operation).
  • To deliver push notifications (notifications are sent only for app features/updates; you may opt out via your device settings).

5. Data sharing

We do not share user personal data with third parties. The only data exchanged is with third-party SDKs (AdMob, Firebase) as described above — these SDKs may collect data pursuant to their own privacy policies. We do not sell or otherwise transfer personal user data to advertisers, brokers, or other third parties.

6. Ads & ad personalization

Ads are provided by Google AdMob. You may see personalized ads based on the information collected by Google. To opt out of ad personalization, users can visit Google Ad Settings at adssettings.google.com.

7. Notifications

The app uses push notifications to deliver important updates or features. Users can disable notifications at any time in the device settings (Android: Settings → Apps → CalmSpace Pro → Notifications).

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data accessible to the app. However, no internet transmission or storage can be guaranteed 100% secure — use the app at your own risk.

9. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected personal information from a child, please contact us and we will take steps to remove the information.

10. Data retention

Since the app does not collect or store personal user data, there is no personal data retention by the developer. Any analytics or crash data retained by third-party services (Google/Firebase) are handled by those services according to their policies.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will post the updated Privacy Policy with a new effective date. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
