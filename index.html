CalmSpace — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

CalmSpace ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Personal Information Collected

CalmSpace does not require an account, and we do not collect personal data such as name, email, phone number, address, photos, or contacts. You can use the app fully without providing any personal details.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. Firebase may collect:

  • App usage events (e.g., starting timer, selecting sound)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (country/region only)
  • Crash logs

This data is anonymous and cannot identify you personally.

AdMob (Google Mobile Ads)

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, device information, ad interactions, and approximate location to serve relevant ads. For details, see Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

2. How We Use the Information

We use analytics and ad-related information for:

  • Improving app performance
  • Identifying and fixing bugs
  • Understanding which features are used
  • Delivering ads via AdMob

We do not sell or share your information with third parties other than service providers (e.g., Firebase, AdMob) who process data on our behalf.

3. Data Storage & Security

We do not store personal data on our servers. Firebase Analytics stores aggregated, anonymous usage data. We follow reasonable security practices to protect data.

4. Children’s Privacy

CalmSpace is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Permissions We Use

CalmSpace may request the following permissions:

We do not access location, contacts, camera, gallery, or microphone.

6. Third-Party Services

CalmSpace uses the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Crashlytics (if enabled)

7. Your Choices

  • Turn off personalized ads from your device's Google settings.
  • Uninstall the app at any time to stop all data collection.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be published in the app or on our website.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: calmspace.app.help@gmail.com.
